OPSEC – Operational Security in the Digital Age

What is OPSEC?

OPSEC is the process of identifying, assessing, and protecting critical information from exploitation. It’s not just tools—it’s mindset, discipline, and lifestyle.

🧠 Core OPSEC Principles

  • Don't overshare (online or offline)
  • Segregate identities: use alias, burner emails, and different devices
  • Only connect to trusted networks; avoid public Wi-Fi or use secure VPNs
  • Be invisible digitally—turn off location, permissions, and telemetry

🛠️ Tools to Support Your OPSEC

  • GrapheneOS – Hardened OS for Pixel phones
  • RethinkDNS – App firewall & DNS-based blocking
  • Kuketz Blog – Deep dives into mobile privacy
  • Proton Mail & VPN – Secure Swiss-based services
  • Tor Browser – For anonymous browsing

📦 Compartmentalization Techniques

  • One SIM = One Purpose = One Account
  • Isolated logins, VPN tunnels, and browser containers
  • Use offline tools (KeePass, Aegis, etc.) for credentials and codes

  • Audit your phone and digital behavior weekly
  • Uninstall unnecessary apps; disable permissions
  • Practice offline days and device disconnection
