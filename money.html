Money = Privacy – Control the Flow, Control the Risk

💵 Why Financial Privacy Matters

Your financial footprint can expose your entire life—habits, patterns, intentions. To control exposure, control your money's movement.

🏦 Offline Banking Tactics

  • Choose banks without biometric-heavy policies
  • Prefer banks like IPPB with branch visits, minimal surveillance
  • Withdraw/Deposit at counters instead of ATMs
  • Use passbooks and avoid mobile banking apps

🪙 Cash Is King

  • Use cash wherever possible, break it into parts
  • Buy essentials in cash, avoid loyalty cards
  • Keep small bills for daily purchases
  • Hide emergency cash in analog safe spots

📱 Digital Payments with OPSEC

  • Use UPI with alias accounts, non-primary SIMs
  • Use non-KYC wallets for one-time payments
  • Disable SMS, location & permissions for finance apps
  • Use sandboxed app profiles (like Shelter, Island)

🧰 Tools for Private Finance

  • Privacy.com – US-based card masking
  • Monerujo – Mobile wallet for Monero
  • Cash App – Peer payments with privacy controls

📚 Learning More

  • LocalMonero – Buy/sell XMR anonymously
  • Dark.fail – Directory of privacy-focused hidden services
  • Bisq – Decentralized crypto exchange

💡 Suggestions

  • Store transaction logs offline only
  • Have a system: cash for daily, UPI for rare needs, crypto for anonymous trades
  • Rotate usage patterns and don't build habits
